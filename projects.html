Projects & Prototypes

I build small, focused tools and workflows to test ideas quickly: AI-assisted knowledge helpers, low-code apps, and decision-support concepts. The goal is always the same — make responsible digital transformation real and usable.

Responsible AI / Data Accountability Assistant

A lightweight assistant concept to help managers document why a certain dataset or metric was chosen in a “responsible” initiative, and what risks were considered. The point: force clarity before decisions disappear into PowerPoint.

Stack: chat-style interface + structured logging.

Research Knowledge Retrieval Tool

A retrieval-style helper for pulling relevant academic insight, policy language, and ethical guidelines on demand — so people making decisions can ground their choices in more than “gut feel”.

Stack: semantic search + curated sources.

Decision Support Cheat Sheets

One-page guides that translate academic language (governance, accountability, sustainability, etc.) into fast questions a manager can actually answer in a meeting.

Format: PDF / slide-ready / copy-paste friendly.
