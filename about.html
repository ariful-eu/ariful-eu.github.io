Research interest

What I work on

I’m interested in how organizations claim to be “responsible,” and what that actually means in practice. Who decides what is ‘responsible’? What data counts as evidence? Who gets left out?

Methodologically I combine qualitative work (manager interviews) with conceptual framing from information systems, governance, and sustainability.
